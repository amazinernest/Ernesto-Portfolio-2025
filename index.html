Developer, designer, and creative problem-solver.

I am a developer with a passion for crafting accessible, high quality user interfaces where design meets engineering. I enjoy building digital experiences that do not just look beautiful but are also purposeful, performant, and user friendly.

Currently, I serve as a Senior WordPress Developer at AscendWP, where I focus on accessibility. I help design and maintain UI components that shape the platform’s frontend, making sure every feature aligns with web accessibility standards and delivers an inclusive experience for all users.

Over the years, I have worked on websites and products across diverse spaces from startups and agencies to large organizations, sharpening both my technical and design instincts. I also create YouTube courses on tech and AI, sharing knowledge and practical insights with learners looking to grow their skills.

Outside of work, you will likely find me reading, playing football, spending time with friends, or chasing after whatever sparks my curiosity next.
